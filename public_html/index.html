    Rubyfuza is the only African conference for Ruby developers. The event comprises of two days of single-track presentations and an additional workshop day. The organizers of Rubyfuza 2016 is encouraging the submission of proposals for 30-minute presentations OR workshops related to Ruby development. Sessions may be geared toward non-expert audiences or focus on topics relevant to experienced developers. The deadline to submit proposals is December 31, 2015.
